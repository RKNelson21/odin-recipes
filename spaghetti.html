Spaghetti Recipe

Introduction

This is probably the best spaghetti that I have ever had in my entire life!! Lets get to it!

Ingredients

All of these ingredients can be found at your local grocery store.

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • McCormick Thick and Zesty Spaghetti Seasoning
  • 2 12 oz cans of Hunt's Tomato Paste
  • 14 - 18 fl oz of water
